Desire for the path of hope

Desire for the path of hope tells a story of three disabled persons, who have difficulties in making a living and integrating into society: A disabled female athlete, also a cancer patient, a former journaohlist who lost vision and a Master degree holder who is in a wheelchair.
1. Mai's story: The “Queen” Athlete
2. Quy's story: The untiring footsteps
3. Tung's story: The path to happiness
